Christian Worldview

This page explains some of the core beliefs that shape my Christian worldview and how they influence the way I think, make decisions, and treat other people.

Core Beliefs

  • Purpose: I believe God created the world with purpose and meaning.
  • Truth: I believe moral truth comes from God, not from personal preference.
  • Value: I believe every person has value because they are made in God's image.
  • Grace: I believe forgiveness, grace, and growth are important in daily life.
